About

InnoSphere 2024 in Dar es Salaam is an exclusive AI and Future of Work event, bridging the gap between AI advancements and the evolving nature of work. Hosted by Research Society for IT & AI (ReSITAI) and Greencom Africa, this event will see the convergence of experts, innovators, and thought leaders.

This premier experience at Julius Nyerere International Convention Centre is your chance to engage with industry pioneers, gain deep insights, and establish lasting connections in an environment that melds innovation with collaboration.

Join us as we navigate the horizons of AI, fostering discussions, showcasing applications, and catalyzing collaborative efforts. Welcome to InnoSphere!

About Us

Research Society for IT & AI (ReSITAI) is a leading research institution focusing on the intersection of information technology and artificial intelligence. Based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, our commitment is to advance the understanding and application of AI in various sectors.

Partnered with Greencom Africa, our initiatives, such as the InnoSphere event, aim to harmonize the dynamic landscape of the future of work with cutting-edge advancements in AI. Together, we aspire to foster insightful discussions, showcase practical applications, and drive collaborative efforts to shape a progressive and sustainable future.
